When new audiences go cold

Why aren't my ads converting with a new audience?

Because the messaging that built trust with your first audience is actively disqualifying the next one. The same lines that reassured cohort A read as evasive, or unserious, or unrigorous to cohort B. I'm Daniel Fox, a fractional B2C CMO. Your trust-builders have become door-closers. The ad mechanics are fine. What's off is who the words are built to reassure.
