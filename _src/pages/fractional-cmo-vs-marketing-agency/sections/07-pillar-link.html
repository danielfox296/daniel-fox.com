The bigger picture

Why it only compounds when one owner runs it.

Choosing between an agency, a freelancer, and a fractional leader isn't really a hiring question. It's a question about how demand gets built for a purchase people think hard about before they make it. Search, content, AI visibility, paid, capture, and nurture only compound when they're pointed at the same plan by the same owner. Split them across vendors who each optimize their own slice and you get activity that cancels itself out.
