What it costs, and what it returns

How much does a fractional CMO cost?

It depends on scope, but here is the anchor: most fractional CMOs run $8,000 to $22,000 a month, and mine start at $10,000. I'm Daniel Fox, a fractional B2C CMO. The more useful question is not the rate. It is what the seat returns against what a full-time hire, or the unled spend you are already carrying, actually costs you.
