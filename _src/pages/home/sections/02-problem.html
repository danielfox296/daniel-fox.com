The situation

Over-spending, under-led.

The business is profitable and the budget is real — ads, a site, a feed, maybe an agency. What's missing is a senior brain pointing it. Someone optimized the dashboard mechanics without ever asking whether the spend points the right message at the right person.

Activity, not strategy

Posts go out, ads run, the numbers move sideways. There's motion, but no one owns the plan it's supposed to serve.

Budget without a conductor

Real money flows across channels every month, routed through whoever has a minute — usually the owner, between everything else.

It lands back on your desk

Every decision — message, media, vendor — comes back to you. You wanted someone to take it and run. You never found them.
