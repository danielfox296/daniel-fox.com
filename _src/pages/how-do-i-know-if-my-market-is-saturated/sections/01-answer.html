Saturation vs. wrong address

How do I know if my market is saturated?

From the data alone you usually can't, and that's the trap. A saturating market and a market you're addressing wrong produce the same silence: no complaints, no friction, just fewer sales. I'm Daniel Fox, a fractional B2C CMO. The wrong market won't tell you they're the wrong market. You separate the two by going to people, not dashboards.
